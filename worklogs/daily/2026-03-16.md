## MBF coordination-log enrichment + tc_* reverse mapping

**Why.** **MBF** = Mobifone, Vietnamese telco; resuming the scoring production. PM asked us to review the data scheme between the TS scoring pipeline and MBF's new data scheme — pre-contract check-up tracked under [DCI-328](https://trustingsocial1.atlassian.net/browse/DCI-328) (sub-task of [DCI-321 Telco Score Maintenance](https://trustingsocial1.atlassian.net/browse/DCI-321)). The ticket itself was filed retroactively on Apr 22 to capture this Mar 16-20 workstream. Today opens **Round 1**: the goal is to produce a side-by-side of "what MBF can deliver" vs "what our `tc_*` clean-data pipeline reads", so we can request the right sample for the next round.

The MBF **Coordination Log** is the upstream spreadsheet listing every Vietnamese-named field MBF can deliver, with `STT` (row id) and `Mức độ chi tiết tối đa` (max-frequency / aggregation grain). The `tc_*` clean-data tables (e.g. `tc_accounts`, `tc_call_histories_contact`, `tc_balance_histories`) are the downstream side our score-pipeline already reads.

> ### 🛠 What I did
>
> - **Wrote `enrich_csv.py`** — enriches the MBF Coordination Log with three columns (`required`, `english_name`, `source_table`) using a hand-curated `STT → (required, english_name, source_table)` mapping. Populated ~80+ STT rows in this initial pass: e.g. STT 1 → `phone_number` / `tc_accounts`, STT 17 → `creation_date` / `tc_balance_histories`, STT 22 → `called_phone_number` / `tc_call_histories_contact`. The `required="No"` rows mark MBF-only fields with no TS counterpart — these become the "drop or derive" decision queue.
> - **Wrote `reverse_mapping.py`** — first cut of the inverse pass: for each column in every `tc_*.csv`, look up the matching Vietnamese field name + frequency from the enriched log. Output schema: `name_csv | column_name | data_sample | vietnamese_name | frequency | covered`. Encoded **two `TABLE_ALIASES` rules** for known semantic-equivalent pairs: `tc_accounts_deactivation_post → tc_accounts_deactivation_pre` (post-paid deactivation reuses pre-paid field semantics) and `tc_callfw → tc_call_histories_contact` (call-forward records share the call-contact-history schema).
> - **Re-authored as `reverse_mapping_v2.py`** — same-day rewrite, ~3.4× the size of v1; folded in the v1 logic plus extended handling. v1 retained as the simpler reference implementation.
> - **First read of the legacy `mbf_cs_airflow_202208` monorepo** under `mbf/mbf_csv4/` — same lineage that the Mar 24 schema-audit project re-extracts. Today's pass was orienting on the CSV2/CSV3/CSV4/ISV1 dev trees; no edits.

**Result.** **Round 1 prep complete on day-1.** Two scripts (enrich + reverse-map) plus a v2, all authored in one focused burst, give a concrete pair of CSVs to answer *"which TS `tc_*` columns are MBF-covered, and which Vietnamese fields have no TS home?"* — the side-by-side that lets us request the right sample. Sets up the sample-data review that runs Wed (censored sample arrives) and Fri (encrypted sample + encrypted-baseline run).
