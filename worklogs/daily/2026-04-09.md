## AGI-6751 · Master Call Code

**Why.** Following the Mar 23 customer-vs-call-receiver clarification, two open inconsistencies remained in the master mapping. **(1) `N/A` cells** in the Affirm column flagged codes Affirm doesn't anticipate (e.g. `RIN`) but which still ship in the postcall classifier prompt — wasted prompt tokens *and* added misclassification surface area for codes that have no canonical destination. **(2) `DIM` (Debt Sale 2025)** lacked a clear surface signal for triage, blocking AGI-9466 AC-002 routing.

> ### 🛠 What I did
> Posted two JIRA comments:
> - **(1) NA-code rationalization** — asked whether codes Affirm doesn't anticipate could be dropped from the prompt to save tokens, and how to route conversations whose mapping isn't available.
> - **(2) DIM follow-up** — requested the surface signal that triggers `DIM`.
>
> *(JIRA comments, 2026-04-09 14:23 + 17:51 +0700)*

**Result.** Same-day responses from Huong Bui resolved both:

- **DIM definition pinned** — triggers when "a customer has questions regarding the sale of their loans to a third-party buyer and is transferred to a human agent." This is the wording that flows into AGI-9466 AC-002 (`DIM → DIM` routing).
